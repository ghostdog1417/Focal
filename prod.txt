If your question is still about what to change for proper signed APK, change these exact fields:

File: key.properties:-
1. storePassword: change to your real password
2. keyPassword: change to your real password
3. k yAlias: keep as upload (or your alias)
4. storeFile: path to your keystore, currently ../upload-keystore.jks

File: build.gradle.kts:-
1. Already wired correctly to read key.properties
2. No further change needed unless you move/rename keystore

File: android\upload-keystore.jks:-
1. Replace this keystore if you want your own production key
2. If you replace it, keep alias/passwords aligned with key.properties

Then build again:
flutter build apk --release
